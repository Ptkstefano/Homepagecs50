Island Tapes


Island Tapes is the first game I made from beginning to end. It started out as an exercise on implementing audio for an immersive open-world, but I ended up developing a little story for it and even hired a voice actor!

It's a very short walking simulator that can be beaten in around 10 minutes and while barebones I'm happy with the end result for a learning project. It can be downloaded for free here.

Pixel Taxi (wip)


Pixel Taxi is a game I'm working on right now about driving a little isometric taxi through a pixel art city. This started as an experiment since I don't think there are really any games with this aesthetic other than maybe Rock n' Roll Racing and Power Drive for the SNES and I wanted to see if it could ever work. It turns out to drive really well. Now I'm building a game on top of this mechanic.

There's a lot of work to do but a playable state is somewhere in the horizon!
